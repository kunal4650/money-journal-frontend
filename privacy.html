Privacy Policy

Last updated: Today

1. Data Collection

We only collect the data you explicitly provide: your name, email, security question, and the financial entries you log. We do not connect to your real bank accounts.

2. Data Security

Your password and security answer are securely hashed using bcrypt before being saved. We use JWT (JSON Web Tokens) to secure your active sessions. No one, not even the administrators, can see your raw password.

3. Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties. Your data is stored securely via Supabase cloud infrastructure.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to export your data at any time via the Settings page. If you wish to delete your account entirely, please contact the administrator.
