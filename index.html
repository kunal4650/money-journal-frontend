Frequently Asked Questions

Is MyExpenseJournal safe to use?
Yes. We take security seriously. We use hashed passwords and secure JWT tokens for authentication. Furthermore, we never ask for your real bank login details or credit card numbers. All data entry is manual and private.
Is this platform really free?
Yes! Tracking your personal finances shouldn't cost you money. The core features of MyExpenseJournal are completely free to use.
Can I delete my data if I want to leave?
Absolutely. You can delete any transaction instantly. You can also export all your data to Excel before leaving. We believe in complete user data ownership.
